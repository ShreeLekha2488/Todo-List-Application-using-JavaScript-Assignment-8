Kovai Tourist Places

Adiyogi Shiva Statue & Isha Yoga Centre:

A massive 112-foot face statue of Shiva located in the Velliangiri Mountains, offering a peaceful, spiritual atmosphere.

Marudhamalai Murugan Temple:

A 12th-century hilltop temple dedicated to Lord Murugan, located on the Western Ghats, famous for its architecture.

Siruvani Waterfalls & Dam:

Located near the Siruvani Hills, this waterfall offers pure water and a serene, scenic spot.

Perur Pateeswarar Temple:

A 1000-year-old temple dedicated to Lord Shiva, known for its Dravidian architecture and the 'Kanaka Sabha' adorned with gold.

Gedee Car Museum:

A popular museum displaying a rare collection of vintage cars from Germany, France, and the UK.

Eachanari Vinayagar Temple:

A significant temple built in 1500 AD, known for its large Ganesh idol.

Valparai:

A hill station near Pollachi with tea estates and panoramic views of the Anamalai Hills.

Anamalai Tiger Reserve (Indira Gandhi Wildlife Sanctuary):

A vast nature reserve home to tigers, elephants, and rare flora, perfect for eco-tourism.

Kovai Kondattam:

An amusement park featuring water rides, family activities, and entertainment.
